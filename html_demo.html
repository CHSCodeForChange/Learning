CHS Code For Change

Innovate. Educate. Serve.

CHS Code For Change is a club at Carmel High School in Carmel, Indiana. Our goal is to employ the programming talent at our school to work on innovative coding projects that benefit the community and local organizations, while also developing skills that will help students in the future.
